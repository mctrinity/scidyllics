About Me

Hello there! I'm Maki Dizon, a web designer and Python developer with a passion for all things digital. Beyond the code and design, I'd like to share a bit of my personal journey with you.

My path into the tech world was a serendipitous one. I made the leap from being a DevOps Engineer to follow my heart and pursue my creative side in web design and development. It's been a thrilling ride filled with endless learning and growth.

When I'm not in front of the computer, you'll often find me in the kitchen, whipping up a delicious pasta dish or making the perfect Ruben sandwich. Cooking is not just a hobby for me; it's a form of creative expression and a way to unwind.

On lazy weekends, I love immersing myself in the vast world of Skyrim, going on epic adventures and conquering dragons. I also have a soft spot for nostalgia and can't resist watching reruns of "The Golden Girls" with a bowl of popcorn.

Baking is another one of my passions, and there's nothing quite like the aroma of a freshly baked apple pie filling the kitchen. It's a delightful way for me to combine my love for creativity and food.

As a self-taught enthusiast, I cherish the opportunity to connect with fellow learners and share knowledge. Whether it's web design, Python development, or just a friendly chat about our favorite games or recipes, I'm here to connect and explore the exciting world of technology and beyond together.

Thank you for visiting my page, and I look forward to getting to know you better as we embark on this journey together!
